psbop@CS-CCR-SPS1
*** COMMENTS FROM MEA on 20/07 ****

As explained yesterday by email, the rotators used were the wrong ones.

The SDDS D2 Ch1 rotators where those used to setup the cavity servoloop, one is NOT supposed to touch them again otherwise the servoloop will not work in a good way or, if the rotators are changes too much, it will not work at all.

This might result in excessive voltage being required from the cavity, with the risk of damaging the amplifiers.

I mean, to align the C04 with the C02 one would not go and touch the tuning and voltage feedback loops implemented in the HLRF, why the hell would one want to do that for the Finemet system, where the servoloops are implemented in the LLRF insetad of in the LLRF?

If you look in the synoptics shown in an event above this one you can see that there is a dedicated rotator for aligning the Finemet and the C02. These are the parameters to be used for the alignment.

(annoyed) MEA
